SWRL rules

  • NegativReview
  • NeutralReview
  • PositiveReview
  • VerifiedReview

NegativReviewback to ToC or SWRL ToC

A review is negative when the overall rating is smaller than 5

Review c(?r) , Overall Rating c(?ov) , has_rating op(?r,?ov) , has_ratingValue dp(?ov,?v) , lessThan(?) -> Negative Review c(?r)

NeutralReviewback to ToC or SWRL ToC

A review is neutral when the overall rating is greater or equal 5 and less or equal 7

Review c(?r) , Overall Rating c(?ov) , has_rating op(?r,?ov) , has_ratingValue dp(?ov,?v) , lessThanOrEqual(?) , greaterThanOrEqual(?) -> Neutral Review c(?r)

PositiveReviewback to ToC or SWRL ToC

A review is positive whe the overall rating is greater than 7

Review c(?r) , Overall Rating c(?ov) , has_rating op(?r,?ov) , has_ratingValue dp(?ov,?v) , greaterThan(?) -> Positive Review c(?r)

VerifiedReviewback to ToC or SWRL ToC

A Review is verified if their authors were actually passengers of the flight, therfore the author has to have a booking.

Review c(?) , has_itemReviewed op(?,?) , has_author op(?,?) , has_arranged op(?,?) , has_reservation_for op(?,?) -> Verified Review c(?)
